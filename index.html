About

McMaster Climbing Club brings together students who enjoy movement, challenge, and supportive peer learning. Our sessions prioritize safety, respectful coaching, and inclusive participation.

Whether you are trying climbing for the first time or already training regularly, you will find a community that values skill development, teamwork, and responsible use of the campus climbing wall.
